Autism


Autism is a complicated condition that includes problems with communication and behavior. This makes it hard for them to express themselves, either with words or through gestures, facial expressions, and touch. So next time, when conversing with them, a bit of patience goes a long way!
Dyslexia


All kids struggle at times to pay attention, listen and follow directions, sit still, or wait their turn. But for kids with ADHD, the struggles are harder and happen more often. This may make them inattentive, hyperactive, impulsive. Hence, when interacting with them, give them more leeway and be more forgiving! They are just like us after all!
ADHD


Can you imagine what it would be like to not be able to walk properly, much less running and jumping? Muscular Dystrophy is a condition which lead to muscle weakness and degeneration. This makes their everyday tasks extremely hard to do. Thus, if ever you encounter a person with muscular dystrophy, never hesitate to lend them a helping hand!
