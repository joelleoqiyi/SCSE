Ears Up!

While you were doing your course, EXA was listening to your remarks...

EXA picked up the following words:

  • Shit
  • What the heck
  • What is happening

Well...you only did the course once. Imagine someone with Dyslexia having to go through the same thing every single day.
How would they feel?
So next time, let's be more understanding to them!
